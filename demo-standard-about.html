Our Story

From lantern light to city nights

Same fire. Better seats. The rest is just time and practice.

How it began

Moon Tim’s started as a roadside grill lit by lanterns on the edge of town — a few cast-iron pans, a small oak fire, and a stubborn belief that good steak didn’t need to be complicated.

Over the years the shack grew into a dining room, but the heart stayed the same: fire, patience, and honest flavor.

How it feels now

Today we’re the place date nights end up, celebrations land, and visitors get sent when locals say, “Go somewhere worth it.”

Warm lighting, comfortable booths, and a menu that keeps the focus where it belongs.
