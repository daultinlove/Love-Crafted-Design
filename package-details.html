Package breakdown

Clear deliverables, timelines, and what to expect from each tier.

Starter — Launch

Best for new businesses that need to look legit fast.

  • 1–3 pages (Home, About, Services/Contact)
  • Basic SEO setup
  • Google Business Profile setup
  • Contact form or click-to-call
  • 1 revision round
  • Handoff training video

Timeline: 5–7 business days after kickoff + content.

Standard — Stand Out

The “most businesses should pick this” package.

  • Everything in Starter
  • Up to 7 custom pages
  • Simple logo suite (primary + alternate)
  • Brand colors & typography
  • Brand guidelines PDF
  • Business card design
  • Social media templates
  • Booking system OR menu setup
  • 2 revision rounds

Timeline: 7–10 business days after kickoff + content.

Premium — Full Brand Build

Full identity + best-in-class site and integrations.

  • Everything in Standard
  • Fully custom multi-page site
  • Smooth animations & premium layout
  • Ordering or booking system
  • Stripe payment integration
  • Advanced SEO + content structure
  • Full brand identity system
  • Merch mockups (shirts, hats, stickers, signage)
  • 5 revision rounds
  • 30 days post-launch tweaks

Timeline: 10–20 business days after kickoff + content.
